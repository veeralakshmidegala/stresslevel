Here's what we did..

In various parts of the nation, a student commits suicide once per hour.
According to a 2021 Lacent research, India has documented a significant no.of suicides among young people between the ages of 15-29.

This project focuses on the development of a mental stress dection system using

machine learning, specifically the Decision Tree Classifier.

The dataset used in this research was collected from kaggle and remodified that data,which was use for us.

Participants were asked a series of questions pertaining to their emotional state and reactions in various situations they may have encountered.

These answers were assigned weight to calculate a stress score, enabling a comprehensive analysis of individuals stress levels.
